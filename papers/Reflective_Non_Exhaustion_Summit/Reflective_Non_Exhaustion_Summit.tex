\begin{abstract}
This short paper states the summit-level \emph{reflective non-exhaustion} thesis in
clear scope: canonical certification does not exhaust realization when comparison is
tracked on the right carriers. We distinguish the claim from G\"odel--style barrier
theorems, from generic dependent-type proof relevance, and from informal slogans about
``consciousness'' or AGI. Routes C/A/B/D label how the thesis is supported internally,
in applications, and in computability. For the map of manuscripts and discharges,
see~\cite{SpivackSynthesis}.
\end{abstract}

\section{Reflective Non-Exhaustion (summit statement)}

\textbf{Thesis.} In structured reflective settings, a principal certified invariant at a
bare carrier can coexist with nontrivial realization data above that carrier: the
forgetful comparison map is sound but need not be injective, and multiplicity can be
organized in fibers with explicit obstructions to trivializing lifts.

\noindent
The Lean artifact instantiates this thesis with named definitions and theorems; the
flagship paper~\cite{SpivackIC} is the internal mathematical origin.

\section{Broad structural class}

The class of phenomena is not tied to a single domain: collapse/refinement/fiber appears
in the internal library~\cite{SpivackIC}, in twelve external benchmarks~\cite{SpivackValidation},
and in algebra/topology discharges~\cite{SpivackFiber,SpivackQuillen}. What unifies them is
\emph{packaging}: the same layered analysis applies when $\pi$, fibers, and sections are
made mathematically explicit.

\section{Exact scope}

This summit paper is \textbf{not} a substitute for formal statements in Lean. It does not
add new axioms. It does not assert that every formal system or every epistemic agent must
exhibit non-exhaustion. It summarizes a research program whose precise carriers and
hypotheses are documented in the repository and companion manuscripts.

\section{Distinction from G\"odel}

G\"odelian incompleteness exhibits \emph{limits} of formal systems (undecidable sentences,
non-recursive theories, etc.). The present program exhibits a \emph{positive} stratification:
where collapse occurs, where it does not, and how obstruction is organized. Route~D aligns
computability anchors with the classical story, but reflective non-exhaustion is not
``G\"odel's theorem in a new notation.''

\section{Not merely dependent-type proof relevance}

Dependent type theory admits proof-relevant types; that observation alone is not the
contribution. The contribution is a \emph{theorem} about specific carriers in a
machine-checked library: collapse at $\mathcal{B}_{\mathrm{can}}$, strict refinement of
$\pi_A$, and fiber lemmas with the hypotheses used in development~\cite{SpivackIC}.

\section{Why it matters across fields}

Algebra: cocycle obstructions to splitting. Topology: contractible fibers in Quillen's
hypotheses. Arithmetic: embedding problems as liftability. Verification: audit trails
above bare certificates. The same architectural lens travels because the layers are
mathematically standard once $\pi$ and fibers are fixed.

\section{Self-modeling, consciousness, and AGI (delimited)}

Any analogy to self-modeling or subjective experience is \textbf{speculative} and
\emph{not} a theorem of this program. What \emph{is} formalized are mathematical carriers
and comparison maps in Lean. Philosophical reading is optional; the summit claim stands
on the formal discharges and the validation survey.
